Wixton Candle Company was created by Daisee Fuentes. Daisee wanted to create long-lasting candles that could fill a whole room with an amazing scent. She spent months researching and testing products to find the combination that she felt worked the best. The result: a coconut soy wax with a wood wick. The coconut soy wax provides a cleaner burn than other waxes such as paraffin. Next, the wood wick was added to provide a crackling-effect that simulates a fireplace when the candle is burning. Each product is hand-made and tested before it is sold. Wixton Candle Co. is based out of Fresno, CA where everything is made and shipped from.
